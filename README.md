Hello! Welcome to my simple mod!

This is really just to solve an annoying logistics problem:

LIQUIDS CAN'T GO INTO MASS LAUNCHERS OR DRONES!

In the future, I may add liquid-specific long-range transportation,

but for now we will just convert liquids to items and back again.

Installation:
* Open "mods" in game
* Open "import from GitHub"
* Write: StrawbreeCake/misc-utility
